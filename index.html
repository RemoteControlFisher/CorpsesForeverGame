• AD or arrow keys to move (WASD Reccomended.) Left Shift or J to sprint.
  • Space, w, or up arrow to jump. Hold jump to jump higher. (Short hop is 2 blocks tall, tall hop is 3 blocks tall, and special hops are 1 block tall.)
  • Down while moving to slide.
  • Jump while sliding for a special high speed horizontal jump.
  • Jump with your face against the wall to wall jump (Hint: The wall must be a certain height to allow wall jumps).
  • Press down while in the air or standing still to fall through soft platforms (Boxes, corpses.)
  • Picking up and throwing the corpses is not implemented yet...
  • Hint: use the corpses in the first room to escape.
